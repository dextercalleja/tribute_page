Dale Carnegie

An American Writer and Lecturer

Timeline

1888 Born in Maryville, Missouri
1908 Graduate college in Warrensburg
1911 Carnegie had saved up $500, which was enough to quit his job, move to New York City and try to make it as an actor
1913 Published his first book, Public Speaking and Influencing Men of Business, using it as a textbook for his courses
1931 First marriage ended in divorce
1936 Carnegie published just such a book: How to Win Friends and Influence People
1944 Carnegie married Dorothy Price Vanderpool
1953 Carnegie moved the institute's headquarters into a converted five-story brownstone warehouse in Manhattan
1955 Died of Hodgkin's disease
